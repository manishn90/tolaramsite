Arzu Utama Realty is the developer of Arzuria, a 32-storey upscale residential apartment in central Jakarta occupying a land area of 6,800sqm.

TAN SWEE SIONG  Head - Real Estate

Tan Swee Siong joined the Group in Apr 2006 as Head- Real Estate. He oversees all investment, divestiture, development, management and capital market activities of Tolaram’s real estate portfolio.

He holds a B.Eng (Hons) from the National University of Singapore and obtained his MBA from Melbourne Business School and Johnson Graduate School of Management in Cornell University respectively. Swee Siong is also the holder of Capital Market Service License in “Advising on Corporate Finance”.
