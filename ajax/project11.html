LAGOS FREE TRADE ZONE

Nigeria

Spread over an area of 800 hectares, the Lagos Free Trade Zone (LFTZ) will serve as an industrial and commercial hub for West Africa. LFTZ is strategically located to facilitate trade and boost the economy of Nigeria as well as neighbouring countries in Africa.

KUNDAN SAINANI  Chief Financial Officer

Kundan Sainani joined the Group in 1999 as a Group Internal Auditor. He has over 25 years of experience in industries like steel, paper, textile, chemicals, food sector, infrastructure and construction industry.
He became the Director- Finance for Viva Methanol LFTZ Enterprise in 2003 and moved on to his role as CFO in Lagos Free Trade Zone to take charge for the development of this mega scale integrated industrial park.
He is a commerce graduate and a Chartered Accountant from Institute of Chartered Accountants of India.
