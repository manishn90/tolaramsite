LOTUS INDAH

Indonesia

Lotus Indah Textile Industries located in Indonesia produces Synthetic Spun Yarns from Ring Spinning (100% Polyester, 100% Viscose & Poly/Viscose), Open End Spinning (100% Polyester & 100% Viscose) and Murata Vortex Spinning (100% Viscose) in Single, Double (TFO) & Multifold. It exports to over 30 countries in the world.

GOPAL VENKATESH  CEO

Gopal Venkatesh joined the Group in May 2013 as CEO of Lotus Indah Textiles. Prior to that, he has over 29 years of experience, out of which 12 years of top management experience in various companies in the world.
He holds a Bachelor of Technology and Post-Graduate Diploma in Business Management.
