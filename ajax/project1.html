Tolaram Investment

Singapore

Tolaram Investments AS owns a 10.8 ha industrial site in North Tallinn with approvals to develop the site into a mixed commercial and residential development of 200,000 sq.m. It recently completed the building for a Norwegian-owned supermarket chain named Rimi. It is also the developer of Pagari One, formerly a KGB headquarters, which has been restored as a collection of 42 luxury apartments.

Its subsidiary TH Investments developed and owns a building which houses Hawaii Express Retail and Al Mare Bowling.
