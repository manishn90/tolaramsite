VISHAL TULSIAN  CEO

Vishal Tulsian joined the Group in May 2003 as a Finance Manager. He became the CEO of Horizon Tissue and Horizon Pulp & Paper in 2006. He is currently the Group Head of Business Development in Indonesia.
He holds a degree in Chartered Accountancy from The Institute of Chartered Accountants in India and has completed in Masters in Business Administration from London.
