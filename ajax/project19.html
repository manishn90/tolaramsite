VCONNECT

Nigeria

VConnect is the largest local search engine and ecommerce company in Nigeria. With more than one million businesses listed, it provides extensive information about the products, services and companies in Nigeria.
It was also recognised as the “Hottest Tech Startup” in Africa by Forbes Magazine.

DEEPANKAR RUSTAGI  General Manager

Deepankar Rustagi joined Tolaram Group in Jan 2008, and has worked with Dufil Prima and Lucky Fibres. In 2011 Tolaram Group launched its first venture in digital services called VConnect Global Services in Nigeria and Deepankar was appointed as the business head.
Prior to working in Tolaram he has working in Bangalore with Keane (Boston based IT Services company) as a Business Analyst for 2 years.  He is an Engineering Graduate from Bangalore.
