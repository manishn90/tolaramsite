FIRST CHOICE RETAILING

Nigeria

First Choice Retailing Limited is the largest chain of organised retail stores providing Nigerians with convenient shopping of everyday needs. There are currently 250 outlets under the brand of “Best Choice” in Lagos, with plans to extend it to other cities.


UDAYAN DASGUPTA
General Manager


Udayan Dasgupta joined the group in 2011 in the Best Choice Division of Multi-Pro Enterprises Ltd. He became the General Manager of First Choice Retailing Ltd. in June 2012. He brought a vast amount of experience in Supply Chain Management which he gathered by working in Infosys Technologies Ltd, Bangalore for 4 years.
Udayan has a Post Graduate degree in Management from Indian School of Business, Hyderabad and has completed his B.Tech from Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee in 2006.
